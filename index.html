Welcome

An education built on light, not just marks.

Balaji Public School is a CBSE-affiliated, co-educational school offering classes through the senior secondary (10+2) level. Our classrooms are built for focus, our courtyards for movement, and our stage for the confidence that comes from standing on it. Progress here shows up in report cards — and in the quiet discipline a child carries home.
